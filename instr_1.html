Here are some instructions about how to use the site app properly
Citizens control the possibility vaccination planning sending a text message (SMS) with AMKA (blank) Surname in 13034
Immediately, receive a response from EMVOLIO on the possibility appointment scheduling and are encouraged to schedule appointments
Otherwise, they receive an update from EMVOLIO that they do not yet have the ability appointment scheduling
Request for examination of data in case not finding AMKA
In case that the citizens belong to current vaccination period and no have an AMKA or their AMKA number does not can be recognized by the system, then go to the page "Application examination of data "(emvolio.gov.gr/aitisi).
After completing all necessary fields and its selection request for examination is sent by a competent committee.
Upon consideration of the request, and based on of the decision of the competent committee, will enable them citizens to connect to the platform emvolio.gov.gr and plan it their appointment.
